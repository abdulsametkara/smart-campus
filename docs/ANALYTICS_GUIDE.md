# Analytics & Reporting Guide

## Overview
The Smart Campus Analytics module empowers Administrators to make data-driven decisions. Located in the Admin Dashboard, it provides insights into four key areas.

## 1. Academic Performance
- **GPA Distribution:** Histogram showing grade spread across departments.
- **Pass/Fail Rates:** Pie charts per course.
- **Identification:** Automatically highlights students with GPA < 2.0 (At-Risk).
- **Export:** Download full grade sheets as Excel/PDF.

## 2. Attendance Analytics
- **Trends:** Line chart showing attendance drop-off over the semester.
- **Critical Absence:** List of students exceeding 20% absence limit.
- **Course Engagement:** Compare average attendance across different instructors.

## 3. Meal & Cafeteria (Revenue)
- **Daily Usage:** Bar chart of Lunch vs. Dinner reservations.
- **Peak Hours:** Heatmap showing cafeteria density.
- **Financial:** Daily/Weekly revenue reports from wallet transactions.

## 4. Event & Social
- **Popularity:** Ranking of events by registration count.
- **Participation:** Check-in vs. Registration ratio.

## How to Export
1. Navigate to the specific Analytics tab.
2. Click the "Export Data" button in the top right.
3. Select Format: `PDF` (for printing) or `Excel` (for raw data).
